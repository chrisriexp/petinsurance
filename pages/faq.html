Most Commonly Asked

At what age should I insure my pet?

All Pets over the age of 2 months (8 weeks) and up are elligible for enrollment. We recommend that pets be insured as early as possible to avoid the risk of them developing pre-exisiting conditions that are *not* covered by JustPet.

What determines the cost of my premiums?

The three main factors that determine your premium are your pet's: breed, age and location. Other factors include the selected coverage plan and add-ons to said plan.

When does my coverage go into effect?

Your coverage will go into effect 2 weeks upon signing up. Any injuries/incidents that occur within the waiting period are considered as "pre-exisiting" conditions and will not be covered by JustPet.

Is there a JustPet app?

Our app is currently under construction, however, our website is 100% mobile-optimized! This means you can just add it to your home-screen and it'll be just as efficient as our upcoming app!

Coverage

Does my coverage cover end of life costs?

All of our plans cover euthanasia; however, our base plan does not cover cremation and/or burial.

Do all vets accept my insurance?

Yes, as long as the vetenarian you are visiting is legally licensed in the state it's located it in.

Does JustPet cover dental issues?

Yes, we cover dental AND gum diseases, as well as oral tumors. We do not cover routine dental visits that are considered elective, such as teeth cleaning.

Does JustPet cover Spaying/Neutering?

Yes, we offer coverage for both spaying and neutering, though it is not incluided in our base plans as these are considered elective procedures.

Are there any discounts?

We offer a 10% dicsount for every pet added to your plan!

Are any medical records needed before I can be covered?

Yes, we require the medical reports kept by your main veterinarian so we can ensure your pet receives the most accurate coverage available.
