Coverage

Does Pet Insurance Cover Spaying?

Not all insurances cover exam fees. Our plans cover exams for covered conditions.
Routine wellness exams are not covered by base level plans, and they are only covered under our Preventive Care plans, which you can add on to your base level plans for a little extra cost.
